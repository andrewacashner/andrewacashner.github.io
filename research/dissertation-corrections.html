Edition of Padilla’s Voces, las de la capilla

  • Mr. Stephen Black of the University of Southern California identified several errors of pitches and part labels in the edition of Juan Gutiérrez de Padilla’s Voces, las de la capilla.
  • A PDF of the revised score, typeset as an independent performing score with the text and translation, is available here. This replaces the music edition in appendix C.5, pages 559–569.
  • In musical example 3.5 on page 178, the fourth pitch in the Tenor I voice should be D4, the same as the third note.
