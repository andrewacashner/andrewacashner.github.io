Musical Editions

  • (2018/01/02) I have published a thoroughly revised and corrected edition of several of the villancicos in the dissertation appendix in Villancicos about Music from Seventeenth-Century Spain and New Spain, Web Library of Seventeenth-Century Music no. 32 (2017). Please do not use the poetry or music editions of these pieces in the dissertation except as supplements to the analysis in the main text. The following editions are superseded by the new edition:
    • Jerónimo de Carrión, Si los sentidos queja forman del Pan Divino
    • José de Cáseda, Qué música divina
    • Joan Cererols, Suspended, cielos, vuestro dulce canto
    • Juan Gutiérrez de Padilla, Voces, las de la capilla
    • Juan Gutiérrez de Padilla, Al establo más dichoso
    • Miguel de Irízar, Si los sentidos queja forman del Pan Divino
    • Antonio de Salazar, Angélicos coros con gozo cantad

Chapter 7

  • (2016/05/12) The sentence ending at the top of page 443 contains an error, and the accompanying footnote 29 is incomplete. The author of the archival document on pastoral visitation was not Juan de Palafox y Mendoza, but another priest who worked closely with him, Pedro Salmerón. The citation should be Pedro Salmerón, Apuntamientos para la visita del clero, y ciudad de los Angeles, manuscript, Puebla de los Ángeles: Biblioteca Palafoxiana, R419/074, location no. P3,C348,L2. This document is no. 2526 in the catalog of Palafoxiana manuscripts: Jorge Garibay Álvarez and Jesús Joel Peña Espinosa, Inventario general de manuscritos de la Biblioteca Palafoxiana (Puebla de los Ángeles: Secretaría de Gobierno del Estado de Puebla/Fundación Mapfre Tavera [Madrid], 2004).
