T 9/1. Video Class: What was a nocturne?

Reading

  1. Kallberg (1988)

Listening (on YouTube playlist)

  2. Chopin, Nocturne in C-sharp minor, Op. 27, no. 1
  3. Chopin, Nocturne in G minor, Op. 15, no. 3
