Francesco Algarotti, An Essay on the Opera (1755)

(SRMH 134)

Reading Questions

General

  • What does Algarotti think the goals of opera should be?
  • What does Algarotti think detracts from those goals?
  • How does he think opera should be reformed?
  • What models does he think exemplify good opera?
  • What are the landmarks in the history of opera as he tells it?

Specific

  • (P. 909) What should the poet's role be?
  • (910) What is the relationship of opera to Greek drama?
  • (911) Where did opera start to go wrong?
  • (912) What's wrong with historical subjects? How can they be done well?
  • (914) What's wrong with novelty and fashion?
  • What should be the role of music relative to poetry?
  • What's wrong with...
    • Overtures
    • Recitatives
    • (918) Arias:
      • Vocal parts
      • Instrumental parts
      • Subject and style
      • Relationship between music and words
  • (921) What's good about comic opera? Where does Pergolesi fit in?
  • This is a good time to listen to Pergolesi's La serva padrona.
