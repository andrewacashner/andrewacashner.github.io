    Transcription

    1. From Billie Holiday’s recording of All of Me (below),
      1. Find a lead sheet with the melody and chords (in a fake book or online); or make your own from the recording.
      2. Transcribe Lester Young’s first saxophone solo: First sing it until you’ve learned it, then write it down.
      3. Bring your transcription and your instrument to class Monday.

    Reading

    2. Langston Hughes, The Weary Blues

    Music: Depth

    1. Bessie Smith, Backwater Blues (1927), with James P. Johnson, piano
      1. YouTube video
    2. George Gershwin, I Got Rhythm (from the musical Girl Crazy, 1930): Performances by Gershwin, Judy Garland, Louis Armstrong
      1. Score PDF via IMSLP
      2. YouTube video : Piano solo arrangement, video of Gershwin performing (1931)
      3. YouTube video : Broadway version sung by Judy Garland (1943), big-band swing style
      4. YouTube video : Jazz version (Dixieland/New Orleans style), performed by Louis Armstrong and band (1938)
    3. Gerald Marks and Seymour Simons, All of Me (1931), performed by Billie Holiday and her orchestra with Lester Young, saxophone; swing jazz style
      1. YouTube video
    4. Juan Tizol, arr. Ellington, Perdido (1942; bebop style, based on rhythm changes, i.e., the chords of Gershwin’s I Got Rhythm)
      1. YouTube video : Performance by Duke Ellington and his band
