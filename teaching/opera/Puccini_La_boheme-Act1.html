Listening Guide

Giaccomo Puccini, La bohème

Act I

  • Read the Libretto for Act I.
  • Watch the video of Act I (San Francisco Opera production with Luciano Pavarotti and Mirella Freni).
  • Describe the sets and costumes. Why did the designers make the choices they did, do you think? How do those choices help or hinder the drama?
  • Discuss the acting style of the two principal singers, Luciano Pavarotti (playing Rodolfo) and Mirella Freni (playing Mimì). How is their acting different from the styles you see in other media? Do they both use the same style of acting?
  • In the music, do you notice any recurring patterns?
    • Are some parts more like speech and some more like song? Which? (Use timings from the video or refer to lines in the libretto.)
    • Do you notice any repeating melodies or musical patterns (technically, motives)?
  • Can you identify a few musical elements (use any terms you're comfortable with) that help to differentiate the characters or dramatic moments?
  • What more do you wish you knew to help you understand or enjoy this?
