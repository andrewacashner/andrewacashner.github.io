Primary Sources

  1. The earliest Lutheran hymn book, the Achtliederbuch (1524): Words by Martin Luther and Paul Speratus, tunes by Martin Luther and others, arrangements by Johann Walther

    1. Google Books

    1. Search by title at Hymnary.org

    1. Text translation and tune at Hymnary.org
